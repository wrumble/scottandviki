Map of the area and directions

Reception - From the A26 from Tonbridge turn right onto Bidborough Ridge/B2176. Drive through Bidborough village and stay on the B2176. Once out of the village turn right onto Hayesden Lane signposted Leigh. Juddwood Farm can be found on your right.

Church - Mayfield village is signposted off of the A267, and the church can be found in the middle of the village off of the High Street.

Parking - The car park in Mayfield is free to park!
